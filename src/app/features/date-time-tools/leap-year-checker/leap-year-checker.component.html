📚 Leap Year Logic

  • A year is a leap year if it is divisible by 4.
  • But if it is divisible by 100, it must also be divisible by 400.
  • Leap years have 366 days; non-leap years have 365 days.
  • Leap years keep our calendar in sync with the Earth's orbit.
