📚 About QR Codes

  • QR codes are two-dimensional barcodes that store data.
  • They can encode URLs, text, contact info, and more.
  • Scan with a smartphone camera or QR code reader app.
  • Useful for sharing links, WiFi credentials, and more.

📖 How QR Codes Work

A QR code encodes your input as a pattern of black and white squares, which can be read by a camera or scanner.
